# 🎤 Audibly

**An interactive web app that helps kids build confidence in public speaking and speech therapy through gamified practice, real-time feedback, and progress tracking.**

---

## 🌟 Why Audibly?

Millions of kids struggle with:
- **Public speaking anxiety** — nervousness in front of audiences
- **Speech delays** — pronunciation and articulation challenges
- **Confidence** — not knowing if they're doing it right

**Audibly makes public speaking practice FUN.** 🎮

Kids get instant feedback, earn badges, and watch themselves improve. Parents and speech therapists track progress in real-time.

---

## 🎯 What Audibly Does

### **For Kids:**
✅ **Record & Get Instant Feedback** — Say a word, get feedback immediately  
✅ **Practice Real Scenarios** — Introducing yourselfand giving presentations
✅ **Earn Badges & Points** — Gamified rewards keep kids motivated  
✅ **Fun Characters & Visuals** — Engaging UI designed for kids  
✅ **Privacy First** — Everything stays on your device (or your choice)  

### **For Parents & Therapists:**
📊 **Track Progress** 
📈 **Detailed Reports** 
🎓 **Customizable Lessons** 
👨‍👩‍👧 **Parental Controls**

## 🚀 Features

### **Phase 1: MVP (Launching October 2026)**
- 🎤 Record audio from microphone
- 🔤 Speech-to-text recognition (Google Cloud)
- ✅ Real-time pronunciation feedback
- 🎮 5+ practice scenarios (introducing yourself, show & tell, public speaking, storytelling, etc.)
- 🏆 Badge system (10 badges to unlock)
- 📊 Simple progress tracker
- 👶 Kid-friendly UI with animations

### **Phase 2: Future Updates**
- 📱 Mobile app (iOS/Android via React Native)
- 🎯 Advanced pronunciation scoring (phoneme-level analysis)
- 👥 Multiplayer challenges with friends
- 🎬 Video recording + playback
- 🧠 AI-powered personalized lesson recommendations
- 🌍 Multi-language support

## 🎮 How It Works

1. **Kid logs in** (or starts as guest)
2. **Picks a scenario** (e.g., "Introduce Yourself")
3. **Listens to prompt** (AI reads instructions)
4. **Records their voice** (speaks into microphone)
5. **Gets instant feedback:**
   - ✅ "Great pronunciation!"
   - 🔄 "Good, but you need to be more confident!"
   - 📊 "You've improved 15% this week!"
6. **Earns points & badges**
7. **Parents see progress report**
